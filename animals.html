Peewee

Peewee is known as King of the wallow. When he isn't playing with his bestfriend Jeff, he is often found enjoying a mud bath and will flop for belly rubs.

Peewee
David

David is the newest pig to join the family. He is the smallest of the group with the biggest personality. He is often out playing with the goats.

David
Jeff

Jeff is the smartest pig we've had on our farm. He listens to David Attenborough audiobooks and grunts words like "nomenclature and "esoteric".

Jeff
Kuzco

Kuzco takes after his namesake. He is a very sarcastic alpaca who grew up in luxury. He is usually lazy and wants the others to do everything for him.

Kuzco
Cookie

Just like her name, Cookie is the sweetest gal on the block. She is most likely to give smooches to guests, in hopes of extra treats of course.

Cookie
Juan

Juan is blind in one eye but that doesn't slow him down! He loves twirling in circles and is always the first to greet guests.

Juan
Gandalf

Gandalf is the oldest goat in the group. He spends most of his time dispensing wisdom to the others. He has a loyal heart and a clever mind.

Gandalf
Frodo

Frodo is a brave goat, always jumping off of the highest hills with his bestfriend Samwise. He prefers pets and brushes from guests over treats.

Frodo
Samwise

Samwise is always protecting his trip of goats. He is quiet and shy around guests at first but once he opens up to you, he will shower you with love.

Samwise
Pippin

Pippin is our most cheerful goat, always smiling and prancing around the others. Although a happy fella, he is often up to trouble on the farm.

Pippin
Bilbo

Bilbo is always after the best treats on the farm and is very selective with his food. You will often find him foraging with Gandalf.

Bilbo
Gimli

Gimli is a competitive goat and likes to challenge the others for who can climb the highest hills. He is grumbly in the mornings before he gets his breakfast.

Gimli
Leia

Leia, also known as Princess Leia, is a hard-headed and stubborn donkey, but we know her heart is in the right place. She has a strong sense of teamwork and individualism.

Leia
Poe

Poe is usually going on adventures with Leia, whom he is extremely loyal to. He is a snarky donkey that is usually full of himself, but that comes from being confident in his abilities.

Poe
Mando

When you first come across Mando, you might think he is cold-hearted, when in reality, he is very caring of the others. He has a calming effect on nervous horses that visit the farm.

Mando
Muffin

Muffin is our newest family member at only 3 months old! She spends most of her days with Bobo, her older brother. She likes to eat dandelions and her favourite treat is ryegrass.

Muffin
Kevin

Kevin is an ambitious, adaptable and enthusiastic cow. He is driven and loves to set and accomplish his goals on the farm, which is usually to find the most food he can.

Kevin
Bobo

Bobo, also known as Bo Diddley hasn't met a fence that he can't break. Bobo welcomes affection and is a loving brother to his sister Muffin

Bobo
Norbert

Norbert loves taking dips in the pond and is all about sunbathing. He is often seen running the cows off from eating and appears to perform impromptu karate.

Norbert
Beans

Beans is our gentle giant weighing in at 1,000kg. He loves welcoming folks at the gate of the pasture and sticks around for extra attention.

Beans
Edgar

Edgar is the sweetest of all horses. He is extremely gentle, loving and it ranked #1 in the Province for the most beautiful mane.

Edgar
