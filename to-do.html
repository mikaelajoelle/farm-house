Things to Do

Farm Tour

At the Farm House we offer various activities which allow you to get to know our many residents. Our goal is for you to develop a bond with our animals and hopefully learn a little bit about them. All of our residents have a unique story, and they are all special in their own way. Book a tour with us today to enjoy the full Farm House experience. Our residents can't wait to meet you!

Book Your Tour

Horseback Riding

Horseback riding is one of most popular activities, come ride a beautiful horse and enjoy the scenery of Vancouver. We offer both trail rides and lessons, people of ages and experience levels are welcomed.

Farm Dining

From farm to table, we uses ingredients that we grew by ourselves or purchased from other local farms to create the most delicious vegal meals. Vegan food is bland you say? Well, that is because you have not tried the ones at the Farm House! Make a reservation now and get ready to be blown away! Check out the dining options by clicking the link below.

P.S. Let us know about any food allergies that we should be aware of.

Large Events

Thinking about hosting a birthday bash or wedding? We got you covered! We offer event packages that include catering, decorations, equipments, band performance, and much more. Give us a call at (604)434-5734 to book an appointment with one of our in-house event planners.

Volunteering

We are always looking for volunteers, anyone that is passionate about animals are encouraged to apply. Currently we have open positions for tour guides and administrative assistant. We provide discounted meals and free entrance to the Farm House, this is a great opportunity for animal lovers who want to be part of great team and to make the world a better place.
