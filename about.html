About

Our Story

The Farm House was created in hopes of providing a home and safe space for farmed animals. We are extremely lucky to have such a great team that continues to grow and expand. Our team works extremely hard towards our mission, and we are very thankful to everyone who supports us on this journey.

Our Mission

We aspire to build a better world for our animals in which they can be happy and comfortable. We provide information and knowledge on these wonderful animals to hopefully raise awareness and inspire others to shift their perceptions of farmed animals. Through the various opportunities available at the Farm House we enable and encourage positive interactions between people and the animals. Our mission is to provide a safe space that these animals can call home.

Our Values

At the Farm House, we believe that human and animals are equal. Animals are our best friends and our family, no animal gets left behind on our watch! We've rescued over 50 animals over the years, including pigs, alpacas, goats, and many more. We hope to inform visitors about the cruelty that exists in the farm inustry and to educate everyone how to properly treat the lovely animals on this planet.
